Day 1 (September 25)

Location: Fisher Room (Denys Wilkinson Building) unless otherwise specified

5:00 pm BST Adjourn

~7:00 - 9:20pm BST (finalized times to be confirmed in mid-September) Sprint Dinner at Exeter College (Thanks to the generous support from Breakthrough Listen)

We're having a fancy dinner in one of the colleges ( Exeter College) - traditionally this is an excuse to dress up, but we won't have a dress code. If you'd like to put on something nice, you're welcome. You're also welcome to come in a t-shirt or anything else you like.

Pre-dinner drinks start at TBC. Dinner will be promptly served at 7:30pm

Suggested Pub for Those Interested in Post Dinner Drinks: The King's Arms

Exeter College

The entrance to Exeter College, is through their Porter's Lodge, which is on Turl Street (as shown on this map). Go into to the desk at the Porter's lodge and tell them that you are here for the SSSC Sprint Dinner, and the porters will show you where you need to go.
